Gamification of Github

  • Creation of league based on tickets
  • Weekly or monthly table
  • Response form devlopers
  • Problems with awarding points
This is the first project I worked on during my time here. Keith had the goal that he Wanted to improve coding quality and maybe have a little friendly competition at the same time. (not sure if thats possible based on how the super cup turned out) with this goal in mind he came up with the idea to ‘gamify’ git hub by awarding points to tickets completed creating a league table between developers that would reset weekly or monthly (level playing field) with possibility of prizes for those fishing first. After discussing with John we came up with awarding points based on additions and deletions and time of completion, but after discussing a bit more we realised that this could be easily exploited and after speaking few devs who also thought it couldn’t really be done due to the wide variety of tickets for dev to complete.
